Bespoke Kitchen and Bathroom Solutions About Our Brand

For over 30 years, our success has been built on recommendations and testimonies.

That's because here at Altrac Living, we pride ourselves on giving a truly bespoke service. We offer tailored solutions to meet individual needs, with quality craftsmanship and customer service being our priority. And, as we are designers, manufacturers and fitters, we are able to offer you this excellent service throughout the whole process.

Our showroom in Middlesbrough showcases many stunning displays which have been designed and built in-house and fitted the team. With a mix of contemporary and traditional looks, we combine different ranges to show you what can be achieved.

We have specialist design consultants who are able to give you ideas, advice and guidance on-site or in your home.

Our experts work with you to get to your perfect design whilst ensuring your individual needs and budget are met. The experienced and highly knowledgable team here at Altrac Living, then get to work manufacturing your chosen design, ensuring the highest quality finish.
